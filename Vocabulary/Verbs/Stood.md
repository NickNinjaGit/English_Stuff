# What is the meaning of the word "stood"
?
* **(Verb - Past of Stand) To Be in an Upright Position:** <span style="color:rgb(0, 132, 255)">Describes the state of being on one’s feet or a position taken on an issue.</span>
* **Synonyms:** <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">remained, stayed, positioned.</span>
* **Portuguese Translation:** <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 80)">ficou em pé, permaneceu.</span>
* **Example:** <span style="color:rgb(255, 255, 0)">"She stood at the window watching the rain." (Ela ficou de pé na janela observando a chuva.)</span>
*The pronunciation is "stʊd." Past tense of “stand.”*
