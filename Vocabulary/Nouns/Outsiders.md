# What is the meaning of the word "outsiders"
?
* **(Noun) People Not Belonging to a Group or Place:** <span style="color:rgb(0, 132, 255)">Refers to individuals who are not part of a particular group, community, or system.</span>
* **Synonyms:** <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">strangers, foreigners, newcomers, outcasts.</span>
* **Portuguese Translation:** <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 80)\">forasteiros, estranhos, de fora.</span>
* **Example:** <span style="color:rgb(255, 255, 0)\">"The town was suspicious of outsiders." (A cidade era desconfiada de forasteiros.)</span>
*The pronunciation is \"ˈaʊtˌsaɪ.dərz.\" Often used socially or culturally.*
